24. "do_convert" doesn't exist

Section: 26.4.1.4 [locale.codecvt] Status: TC1 Submitter: Nathan Myers Opened: 1998-08-06 Last modified: 2018-08-20 12:41:52 UTC

Priority: Not Prioritized

View all other issues in [locale.codecvt].

View all issues with TC1 status.

Duplicate of: 72

Discussion:

The description of codecvt<>::do_out and do_in mentions a symbol "do_convert" which is not defined in the standard. This is a leftover from an edit, and should be "do_in and do_out".

Proposed resolution:

In 26.4.1.4 [locale.codecvt], paragraph 3, change "do_convert" to "do_in or do_out". Also, in 26.4.1.4.2 [locale.codecvt.virtuals], change "do_convert()" to "do_in or do_out".

3377. elements_view::iterator befriends a specialization of itself

Discussion:

The synopsis of the exposition-only class template elements_view::iterator in 24.7.16.3 [range.elements.iterator] includes the declaration "friend iterator<!Const>;". We typically don't depict such friend relationships in the Library specification, leaving the choice of how to implement access to private data from external sources to implementer magic. For consistency, we should strike this occurrence from elements_view::iterator.

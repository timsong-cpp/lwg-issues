852. unordered containers begin(n) mistakenly const

Section: 21.5 [unord] Status: CD1 Submitter: Robert Klarer Opened: 2008-06-12 Last modified: 2018-08-20 12:41:52 UTC

Priority: Not Prioritized

View all other issues in [unord].

View all issues with CD1 status.

Discussion:

In 3 of the four unordered containers the local begin member is mistakenly declared const: 

local_iterator begin(size_type n) const;

Proposed resolution:

Change the synopsis in 21.5.4 [unord.map], 21.5.5 [unord.multimap], and 21.5.7 [unord.multiset]: 

local_iterator begin(size_type n) const;

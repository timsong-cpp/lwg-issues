2129. User specializations of std::initializer_list

Section: 17.5.4.2.1 [namespace.std], 18.9 [support.initlist] Status: WP Submitter: Richard Smith Opened: 2012-01-18 Last modified: 2016-01-28 10:01:27 UTC

View all other issues in [namespace.std].

Discussion:

Since the implementation is intended to magically synthesize instances of std::initializer_list (rather than by a constructor call, for instance), user specializations of this type can't generally be made to work. I can't find any wording which makes such specializations ill-formed, though, which leads me to suspect that they're technically legal under the provisions of 17.5.4.2.1 [namespace.std] p1.

[2012, Kona]

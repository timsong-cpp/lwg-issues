Discussion:

From c++std-edit-873:

17.5.1.2 [headers], Table 11. In this table, the header <strstream> is missing.

This shows a general problem: The whole clause 17 refers quite often to clauses 18 through 27, but D.7 is also a part of the standard library (though a deprecated one).

Proposed resolution:

To 17.5.1.2 [headers] Table 11, C++ Library Headers, add "<strstream>".

In the following places, change "clauses 17 through 27" to "clauses 17 through 27 and Annex D":

  • 1.2 [intro.refs] Normative references/1/footnote 1
  • 1.3 [intro.defs] Definitions/1
  • 7 [dcl.dcl] Library introduction/9
  • 17.4 [description] Method of description (Informative)/1
  • 17.4.2.1.5 [character.seq] Character sequences/1/bullet 2
  • 17.4.2.2 [functions.within.classes] Functions within classes/1
  • 17.4.2.3 [objects.within.classes] Private members/1/(2 places)
  • 17.5 [requirements] Library-wide requirements/1
  • 17.5.1.2 [headers] Headers/4
  • 17.5.4.6 [replacement.functions] Replacement functions/1
  • 17.5.5.4 [global.functions] Global or non-member functions/2
  • 17.5.5.10 [protection.within.classes] Protection within classes/1

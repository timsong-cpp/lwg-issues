830. Incomplete list of char_traits specializations

Section: 20.2 [char.traits] Status: NAD Editorial Submitter: Dietmar Kühl Opened: 2008-04-23 Last modified: 2018-08-20 12:41:52 UTC

Priority: Not Prioritized

View all other issues in [char.traits].

Discussion:

Paragraph 4 of 20.2 [char.traits] mentions that this section specifies two specializations (char_traits<char> and (char_traits<wchar_t>). However, there are actually four specializations provided, i.e. in addition to the two above also char_traits<char16_t> and char_traits<char32_t>). I guess this was just an oversight and there is nothing wrong with just fixing this.

[ Alisdair adds: ]

char_traits< char16/32_t > should also be added to <ios_fwd> in 27.3 [iostream.forward], and all the specializations taking a char_traits parameter in that header.

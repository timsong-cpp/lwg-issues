2761. directory_entry comparisons are members

Section: 27.10.12.3 [directory_entry.obs] Status: NAD Submitter: Ville Voutilainen Opened: 2016-08-03 Last modified: 2016-10-10

Priority: 2

View all other issues in [directory_entry.obs].

View all issues with NAD status.

Discussion:

The comparison operators for directory_entry are member functions (27.10.12.3 [directory_entry.obs]).

That means that a directory_entry can be compared with a path, but a path cannot be compared with a directory_entry, because the left-hand side is not subject to conversions. This seems unfortunate.

Note that the comparisons for path are non-members (27.10.8.6 [path.non-member]).

The meta-p/r is to turn the directory_entry comparisons into non-member functions.

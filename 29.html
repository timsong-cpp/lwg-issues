Priority: Not Prioritized

View all other issues in [narrow.stream.objects].

Discussion:

Sections 29.4.3 [narrow.stream.objects] and 29.4.4 [wide.stream.objects] mention a function ios_base::init, which is not defined. Probably they mean basic_ios<>::init, defined in 29.5.4.2 [basic.ios.cons], paragraph 3.

Proposed resolution:

[R12: modified to include paragraph 5.]

In 29.4.3 [narrow.stream.objects] paragraph 2 and 5, change

ios_base::init

to

basic_ios<char>::init

Also, make a similar change in 29.4.4 [wide.stream.objects] except it should read

basic_ios<wchar_t>::init
